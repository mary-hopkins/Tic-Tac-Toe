Tic-Tac-Toe

The object of Tic Tac Toe is to get three in a row. You play on a three by three game board. The first player is known as X and the second is O. Players alternate placing Xs and Os on the game board until either oppent has three in a row or all nine squares are filled. X always goes first, and in the event that no one has three in a row, the stalemate is called a cat game.

Enter your name as Player 1 and click the X to confirm. If you want to play with a friend, have them enter their name in the slot for Player 2 and click the O to confirm.

If you would like to play against the computer, enter your name as player 1, press X to confirm and then leave Player 2's name blank. Press O to confirm the computer will play against you!

If you want to restart the game, click the restart button.
